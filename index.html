How to Request Account Deletion

Users may request that their LiverWise account—and all associated data—be permanently deleted by sending an email to the address below.

Email for deletion requests:

terryd0612@gmail.com

Include the following in your email:

  • Your account email address
  • Confirmation that you want your account deleted
  • Optional: Any additional information that may help us process your request

What Happens When Your Account Is Deleted?

Once a deletion request is confirmed, the following user-associated data will be permanently deleted from LiverWise servers:

  • Profile information (name, username, email)
  • Profile picture and background image
  • Gallery photos uploaded by the user
  • Recipe submissions
  • Friends list and social connections
  • Scan history and nutrition lookups
  • User preferences and in-app settings
  • Authentication identifiers associated with the account

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

What Data May Be Retained?

Certain data may be retained only when legally required or strictly necessary for fraud prevention, security, or auditing:

  • Server logs (retained automatically for up to 30 days)
  • Purchase receipts or billing records required by Google Play

No personal profile data, photos, or content remains after deletion.

How Long Does Deletion Take?

Once your deletion request is confirmed, we will remove all account-related data within 30 days.
